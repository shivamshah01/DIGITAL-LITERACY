# Stay Safe Online

1. **Never Share Your OTP or Passwords**
2. **Check the Website’s URL**
3. **Never Click Unknown Links**
4. **Use Two-Factor Authentication**
5. **Only Use Trusted Apps**
6. **Check the Sender’s Email**
7. **Change Your Passwords Often**
8. **Never Save Your Details on Public Devices**

---

# Financial Safety

9. **Always Check the Request for UPI**
10. **Never Scan Unknown QR Codes**
